How it works

Who are we?

forestgrey is an urbanized nature initiative working to implement eco-friendly home environments for both flora and fauna.

What do we do?

We work to provide a platform for creating and collaborating upon sustainable urban animal homes, such as apiaries and gardens

How do we do it?

Our innovative natural habitat designs are open-source and implementable for use in real life conservation efforts.
